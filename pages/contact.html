    Frequently Asked Questions

    Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and visiting our campuses.

    We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays. For urgent matters outside these hours, you can reach our emergency contact.

    We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent admissions questions during enrollment periods, we typically respond within 4-6 hours. Phone calls are answered immediately during office hours.

    While walk-ins are welcome during office hours, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure you receive personalized attention and a comprehensive tour. Scheduled visits also guarantee that relevant staff members will be available to answer your specific questions.

    Yes! We offer virtual consultations via video call for prospective students who cannot visit in person. These sessions include course information, virtual campus tours, and Q&A with admissions counselors. Contact us to schedule your virtual consultation.

    Our main Dundori campus offers all engineering courses, automotive mechanics, and computer applications. The Kiondo branch specializes in cosmetology, fashion design, and business courses. However, both campuses can provide information about all programs.

    The main Dundori campus has ample free parking for visitors and students. The Kiondo branch has limited parking spaces available on a first-come basis. We recommend using public transportation or boda boda for the Kiondo location.

    Absolutely! We encourage parents and guardians to accompany prospective students during campus visits. We have dedicated parent resources and can arrange separate sessions to address specific concerns about fees, safety, and career outcomes.

    Our staff can communicate in English, Kiswahili, and several local languages including Kikuyu. If you need assistance in another language, please let us know when scheduling your visit so we can arrange appropriate support.
