Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Nakuru County's premier vocational training center, our journey is one of dedication, growth, and unwavering commitment to practical education.

"Acquisition of Skills for Self-Reliance"

Founded in 2010 with a vision to bridge the gap between education and employment, Dundori VTC began as a small community initiative. Our founders recognized that Kenya's youth needed practical skills that would lead directly to meaningful careers and economic independence.

What started with just 25 students and three instructors has grown into a comprehensive institution serving over 200 students annually across multiple departments. Our success lies in our unwavering focus on practical, hands-on learning that meets real industry needs.

Our Vision

To be the leading vocational training center in East Africa, recognized for excellence in practical skills education and graduate success.

Our Mission

To provide quality, practical vocational training that empowers individuals with marketable skills for sustainable livelihoods and community development.

Our Journey of Excellence

Key milestones that shaped our institution and defined our commitment to practical education excellence.

2010

Foundation & First Steps

Dundori VTC officially opened with 25 pioneering students and 3 dedicated instructors, focusing on basic automotive and welding skills.

2013

Program Expansion

Added Cosmetology and Computer Applications departments, expanding our reach to serve diverse career interests and community needs.

2016

Official Accreditation

Received full accreditation from NITA (National Industrial Training Authority), validating our commitment to quality education standards.

2019

Campus Modernization

Major infrastructure upgrade including new workshops, modern equipment, and expanded facilities to accommodate growing student population.

2022

1000th Graduate Milestone

Celebrated our 1000th graduate, marking over a decade of transforming lives and contributing to Kenya's skilled workforce development.

2025

Digital Innovation Hub

Launching our Digital Innovation Hub with advanced technology courses and online learning platforms to meet evolving industry demands.
