Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about admissions, courses, and student life at Dundori VTC.

The minimum requirements include: being at least 16 years old, having a KCPE certificate or equivalent, basic English and Kiswahili communication skills, and the ability to participate in practical training activities. Some courses may have additional specific requirements.

The complete application process typically takes 7-10 days. We review applications within 24 hours, conduct assessments within 3-5 days, and provide final decisions within 7 days. You'll receive email updates at each stage of the process.

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including monthly installments with no interest charges, semester-based payments, and full payment discounts (5% off). We also have scholarships and work-study programs for qualified students.

Our overall employment rate is 94%, with most graduates finding employment within 3 months of completion. We have partnerships with over 150 local businesses and provide job placement assistance to all graduates.

Course changes are possible within the first two weeks of the program, subject to availability and meeting the requirements of the new course. A small administrative fee may apply, and any fee differences will need to be settled.

While we don't have on-campus accommodation, we maintain a list of approved boarding facilities and homestays near the campus. Our student services team can help you find suitable accommodation within your budget.
